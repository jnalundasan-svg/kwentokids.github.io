Emotions & Connection

Ang Kuwento ni Lola Nena

Lola Nena has lived a long, full life — and she has stories to tell. Through her gentle wisdom, children learn to understand and cope with difficult emotions like sadness, longing, and change. A tender intergenerational story that celebrates the bond between grandparents and grandchildren. When Lola Nena's grandchild feels sad, confused, or angry, she knows just what to do. Through gentle wisdom and unconditional love, she teaches that all feelings are okay. A touching multigenerational story about emotional intelligence and family bonds.

About the Creator

Hi, I'm Kuya Jay! 👋

I'm a Filipino author, illustrator, and educator passionate about creating learning tools that celebrate our culture and help young children grow into kind, emotionally intelligent human beings.

Every book in this store is crafted with love — from the warm, hand-drawn illustrations to the carefully chosen words that speak directly to a child's heart. My goal is simple: to give Filipino children stories they can see themselves in, and to give parents and teachers tools that make meaningful conversations easier.

Whether you're a parent, a lola, a teacher, or a tito/tita — thank you for being here. These stories are for the little ones in your life. 💛

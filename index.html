Language First

Every story is crafted to naturally introduce Filipino (Tagalog) words, phrases, and sentences in a way toddlers can absorb and repeat with joy.

Vibrant Filipino Art

FAQ

Got Questions?

Here are answers to the most common questions from our customers.

What format are the digital storybooks in?
Our storybooks are delivered as high-quality PDF files that can be read on any device — smartphones, tablets, laptops, or e-readers. No special app required!
Are the books only in Filipino?
Our books are bilingual — they feature both Filipino (Tagalog) and English text side by side, making it easy for toddlers and parents to learn together, even if the parents aren't fluent in Filipino.
What age group are the books designed for?
Our current collection is designed for toddlers aged 1–5 years old. We are actively working on expanding to older age groups, so stay tuned for more!
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?
Because our products are digital downloads, we are unable to offer refunds after the file has been downloaded. However, if you experience any technical issues, please contact us and we'll make it right!
Do you ship physical books?
Currently, we offer digital downloads only. This allows us to keep prices affordable and deliver instantly to Filipino families anywhere in the world. Physical books may be available in the future!
How can I stay updated on new releases?
Subscribe to our newsletter below or follow us on social media to be the first to know about new books, promotions, and updates from Kwento Kids!
